Hello! I'm Diogo Moreira. I was born in ’89. I’m from Paraíba, Brazil and I currently live and work here. Currently making plans to see the world.

My hobbies include playing video games, watching drama and sci-fi movies and TV shows, drawing, and listening to and collecting music on physical media (specifically CDs and vinyl). I enjoy soccer and support São Paulo FC. I’ve also been following Formula 1 since 2021. Lately, I’ve been playing and trying to keep up with tennis tournaments as well. I’m a music lover across all genres, but I have a soft spot for Indie and Punk Rock.

I have a degree in Systems Analysis and a master’s degree in Software Engineering. I currently work as a professor at the Federal Institute of Paraíba, where I teach courses related to Web Development and Software Engineering. I’m interested in anything involving technology, and I enjoy engaging in discussions about how it applies to our daily lives and how it shapes them—for better or worse. If you want to know more about my career, you can check my CV.
