In a distant future, a mathematician named Hari Seldon created a new science called **psychohistory** that allows him to predict the future of large populations. Using his predictions, Seldon establishes a foundation to preserve human knowledge and culture as the galactic empire begins to crumble. The series explores themes such as politics, power, and the nature of humanity.
